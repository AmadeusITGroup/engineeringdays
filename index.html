// Session Types

Talk

25min

25 minutes - and not a single more! - to introduce your favourite topic, framework or tool and provide pointers for the attendees to delve deeper on their own.

Long Talk

45min

Present a topic you care about in one of our many tracks. We are open to a wide spectrum of topics, tech or adjacent to tech. Five additional minutes provided for questions.

Deep-dive Talk

120min

Dive at the heart of an exciting topic of yours. This format allows for a comprehensive presentation, which can cover large topics, while allowing many concrete examples.

Hands-on Workshop

120min

Bring your attendees further along with a concrete application of a subject you'd want to share. Attendees come with laptops and have wifi access. You can communicate your pre-requirements ahead of the workshop.

Lunch-time Booth

Lunch

Advertise your community, passion or brand on a dedicated booth during lunch time.
We ask anyone with a booth to strive to make it attractive for our geeky crowd. A table, electricity and logistics support will be provided.

Closing Keynote & Cocktail

Exclusive

Own the grand finale! Amadeus provides the stage and venue, while you bring the inspiring closing keynote & the afterwork party vibes.
